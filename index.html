Frequently Asked Questions

How to buy cars from China?

Contact us via WhatsApp or email. Tell us what brands and models you're looking for. We source directly from factories and 4S dealers across China. We handle all export paperwork, customs clearance, and arrange FOB shipping to your port. Both container and RoRo shipping available.

Which countries do you ship Chinese cars to?

We ship to all left-hand-drive (LHD) markets: Kazakhstan, Russia, UAE, Nigeria, Saudi Arabia, Algeria, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Kenya, Pakistan, Serbia, Georgia, Azerbaijan, Ghana, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman, Iraq, Morocco, Ethiopia, Tunisia, Tanzania, Uganda, Zambia, Zimbabwe, Angola, Rwanda, and more. If your country drives on the left side, we can ship.

What is FOB and how does shipping work?

FOB (Free On Board) means we deliver the vehicle to any Chinese port you choose — Shanghai, Tianjin, Ningbo, Urumqi for rail, etc. From there, you arrange shipping or we can recommend freight forwarders. Transit times: 15-25 days to Middle East, 30-45 days to Africa, 7-15 days to Central Asia by rail/truck.

What documents do I get with the vehicle?

Full export documentation package: Certificate of Origin, Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading, and vehicle compliance documents (certificate of conformity, emission test report). Everything you need for customs clearance in your country.

Are these brand new or used cars?

All vehicles are brand new, factory-warranted, zero kilometers. We are a direct Tier-1 supplier — not a broker. Every vehicle comes with the manufacturer's warranty valid in your market (subject to local distributor policy).

How do I get pricing and stock list?

Message us on WhatsApp or email for our latest VIN-level stock list with pricing. We respond within hours in English, Russian, Arabic, or French. No registration required — just reach out and we'll send you everything you need.
